About

I am currently an associate professor in School of Information, Renmin University of China. Before joining RUC, I did two-year postdoctoral researcher in Department of Computer Science at Purdue University (2017-2019), under the supervision of Professor Xiangyu Zhang; and one-year postdoctoral researcher in School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering at Indiana University (2016-2017), under the supervison of Professor XiaoFeng Wang. I obtained my Ph.D. from Renmin University of China in 2016.

Research Interests

  • Vulnerability Detection
  • Vulnerability Exploitation
  • Malware Analysis
